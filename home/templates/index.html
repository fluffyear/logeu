Logeu!

Logeu is a learning tool which tests your Ancient Greek grammar and vocabulary. As of now, it works with AS vocab, irregular verb vocab and noun endings: it's still an early prototype with more features to come. If you spot a bug or have any queries, please contact me at loguequeries@gmail.com or via Discord, at CEO of meow#0360.
