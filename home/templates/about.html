General stuff

Logeu tests your knowledge of Attic/Classical Greek vocabulary and grammar. Right now, it works only with noun endings, vocabulary from the OCR AS list, and irregular verb vocabulary (which I stole and modified from this page) : it's a WIP with more features to come.

I made this tool, because the fact that Eton had a monopoly on such programmes with the greek software project really annoyed me, plus it doesn't have dark mode.

Explaining stuff

AS Gre -> Eng Vocab -- Choose the correct English definition; OCR AS DVL.

AS Gre -> Eng Vocab Typed -- Type the correct English definition; OCR AS DVL. More likely to generate words which you got wrong, unfamiliar words stored in LocalStorage (in your browser).

AS Eng -> Gre Vocab -- Choose the correct Greek translation; OCR AS DVL.

AS Eng -> Gre Vocab Typed -- Type the correct Greek translation; OCR AS DVL.

Irregular Verb Vocab -- Choose the correct irregular Greek verb corresponding to the English definition, from this page.

Noun Endings -- Choose the correct noun case.

Noun Endings Typed -- Type the correct form of the given noun in the given case.

Verb Accidence -- Parse the verb into tense, mood and voice: currently supports λύω and τίθημι.
