general stuff

Logeu tests your knowledge of Attic/Classical Greek vocabulary and grammar. Right now, it works only with noun endings, vocabulary from the OCR AS list, and irregular verb vocabulary (which I stole and modified from this page) : it's a WIP with more features to come.

I made this tool, because the fact that Eton had a monopoly on such programmes with the greek software project really annoyed me, and I thought I could do a better job (turns out this is not so easy).

explaining stuff

AS Gre -> Eng Vocab -- Multiple choice of English words, OCR AS DVL.
AS Eng -> Gre Vocab -- Multiple choice of Greek words, OCR AS DVL.
Irregular Verb Vocab -- Multiple choice of irregular Greek verbs, from this page.
Noun Endings -- Multiple choice of noun cases.
